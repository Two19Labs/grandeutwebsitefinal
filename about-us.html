The Grandeur Story

Grandeur was established in 2018 at SSCBS to serve as a focused student cell for business consulting, strategy, and research. Recognizing a gap between textbook theories and actual corporate case solving, the founders set up Grandeur to bridge this divide through live industry projects, national case fests, and structured workshops.

Today, Grandeur has grown to become one of the premier consulting societies in the Delhi University circuit. With a track record of national podium finishes and corporate collaborations, we continue to cultivate professional readiness and strategic leadership.

Our Cornerstone Activities

From live corporate advisory to publishing research and hosting national fests, our core activities build a solid foundation of business acumen and consulting preparedness.

Our Vision

To nurture students into future business leaders by bridging the gap between academic learning and corporate reality. We envision a community where every member develops the advisory acumen, strategic thinking, and professional confidence to excel in consulting and general management.

Our Mission

To empower organizations with robust, data-backed strategic insights while offering students invaluable opportunities to work on live consulting projects. We are dedicated to providing business advisory, detailed sector research, and case practice opportunities to foster continuous professional development.
