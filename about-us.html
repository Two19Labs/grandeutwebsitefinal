About SSCBS

Shaheed Sukhdev College of Business Studies (SSCBS) is a premier institution under the University of Delhi. Ranked No. 1 BBA college in India by India Today for six consecutive years, known for its rigorous academics, industry-aligned pedagogy, and vibrant student-led culture, SSCBS cultivates future-ready leaders poised to drive innovation and impact.

The 180DC Story

180 Degrees Consulting was founded with a simple yet powerful idea: to provide high-quality consulting services to organizations that need them most but can least afford them. Our SSCBS branch continues this legacy in Delhi, bringing together students who are passionate about business and social impact.

As part of the world's largest university-based consultancy network, we have access to global best practices, proven methodologies, and a community of like-minded changemakers. This allows us to deliver exceptional value to our clients while providing our consultants with unparalleled learning opportunities.

Our Global Network

Being part of 180+ branches across 30+ countries gives us access to global expertise and best practices in social impact consulting. This worldwide presence enables us to work collaboratively and implement localized solutions derived from international learnings.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth and impact since our establishment.

2018

Founded as the SSCBS chapter of the global 180 Degrees Consulting network.

2019

Onboarded the first set of high-impact clients; launched internal training programs for student consultants.

2020

Completed consulting projects with notable names like Zomato Feeding India and Save the Children.

2021

Expanded client portfolio to 10+ social enterprises and NGOs, including the Covid India Task Force.

2022

Partnered with global organizations like One Acre Fund and CRY for consulting projects.
