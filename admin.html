📘 Guide & Instructions (READ!): Recruitments & Application Portal

Manage candidate recruitment drives, customize application form questions, and review submitted responses.

  • 📌 Default Candidate Details Collected: Every application form automatically asks for Full Name, Email Address, Phone / WhatsApp Number, and logs the Submission Timestamp. You do not need to add questions for these contact basics!
  • 🟢 Portal Toggle Switch: Turn the switch ON to enable the Join Grandeur page link across the website header & footer. Turn it OFF to hide the recruitment link completely.
  • ⏱️ Automatic Form Expiry Cutoff: Set a date & time in the Automatic Cutoff Date & Time field. Once that minute arrives, candidate submissions close automatically and visitors see: "Thank you for your interest, we are not accepting applications anymore" with a button back to home.
  • 📋 Custom Questions Builder: Click ➕ Add Custom Question to create drive-specific questions (text inputs, long paragraphs, or dropdown choices). Questions rendered on the website update live.
  • 📥 Managing Submissions: View all candidate submissions in the card below. After reviewing candidate responses, click 🗑️ Delete Application to clear old records and prevent database limit overruns.
