About Grandeur

Fostering student growth and corporate success through structured strategy consulting, research, and analysis.

Grandeur is the Consulting & Knowledge Cell of Shaheed Sukhdev College of Business Studies (SSCBS), University of Delhi. As one of the oldest and most respected student consulting bodies on campus, we strive to bridge the gap between academic theory and corporate practice by delivering high-impact strategic advisory to corporate firms and non-profits alike.

Our cell combines structured problem-solving, deep sector research, and financial analysis to solve complex challenges. We enable students to develop corporate readiness, leadership traits, and professional competence, driving on-ground impact.
