About 180DC SSCBS

Creating positive change and transforming communities, organizations, and individuals for the better.

180 Degrees Consulting SSCBS is the student-run branch of the world’s largest university-based consultancy. We partner with NGOs, social enterprises, and businesses to deliver innovative, practical, and sustainable solutions to real-world challenges, while equipping students with hands-on consulting experience.

Our team of passionate students combines analytical thinking with creative problem-solving to create meaningful impact. At SSCBS, we strive to empower organizations driving change while fostering continuous learning and growth for our members.
