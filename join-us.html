Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to apply for Grandeur?

Recruitment is open to undergraduate students across all courses (BMS, BBA FIA, B.Sc. Computer Science) and academic batches at Shaheed Sukhdev College of Business Studies (SSCBS).

Do I need prior consulting or financial modeling experience?

No prior consulting experience is required! We value structured thinking, curiosity, communication skills, and a strong problem-solving mindset. Comprehensive internal training and case bootcamps are provided to all inducted members.

What is the expected weekly time commitment?

Members typically dedicate 8 to 12 hours per week to live client engagements, knowledge primers, and internal skill sessions. Time commitment may vary depending on project milestones and competition schedules.

How can I prepare for the selection process?

We recommend familiarizing yourself with basic case structuring principles (such as profitability frameworks, guesstimates, and market entry) and highlighting your analytical experiences in the application form.
